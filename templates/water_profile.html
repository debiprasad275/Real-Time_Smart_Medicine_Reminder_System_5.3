How is the daily total calculated?

  • Age 0–5: 100 ml/kg — WHO infant/toddler reference
  • Age 65+: 30 ml/kg — ESPEN/EFSA geriatric guideline
  • Adults: 35 ml/kg — standard clinical reference
  • Light activity: no adjustment (sedentary / bed-rest)
  • Moderate activity: +15%
  • High activity: +30%
  • Male: +200 ml (EFSA adequate intake difference)
